A Collection of Inspiring Quotes

On Imagination

Imagination is more important than knowledge. For knowledge is limited, whereas imagination embraces the entire world, stimulating progress, giving birth to evolution.

Albert Einstein

On Success

Success is not final, failure is not fatal: it is the courage to continue that counts.

Winston Churchill

On The Future

The future belongs to those who believe in the beauty of their dreams.

Eleanor Roosevelt

Quotes have the power to inspire, motivate, and offer new perspectives. They distill profound wisdom into memorable phrases, encouraging us to reflect on our lives and strive for personal growth.
